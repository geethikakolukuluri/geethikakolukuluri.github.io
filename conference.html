I had the opportunity to present a production-ready iOS application, the PollutionDetectorApp, at the MINK-WIC Conference[cite: 45, 46]. The presentation detailed the end-to-end system architecture, covering modular design patterns, backend API integration, and data processing models. I effectively communicated complex technical concepts, such as real-world data interpretation and metric calculation logic, to a diverse audience of academic and industry stakeholders[cite: 48, 49]. By addressing scalability considerations and modern software engineering best practices, I highlighted the transition from theoretical concepts to cloud-backed application deployment[cite: 49].
